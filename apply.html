Open Positions for MSB 2026

Doctoral Programs Officer (One)

Responsible for planning and executing events, such as PhD Thesis Madness, to help doctoral students showcase their work, network, and receive mentorship. Key tasks for this role include managing submissions, overseeing the review process, and handling logistics for the in-person event. Ideal candidates should be available to attend MICCAI 2026 in Abu Dhabi, UAE, to ensure a seamless and engaging experience.

Scientific Programs Officer (One)

Responsible for organizing and coordinating the MSB EMERGE workshop, including the paper submissions, review process, and workshop logistics, as a satellite event during the conference. Ideal candidates should be available to attend MICCAI 2026 in Abu Dhabi, UAE.

Public Relations Officers (Two)

Responsible for managing the MSB's public relations activities, including social media engagement, to enhance the organization's visibility and reputation. This involves managing MSB social media accounts, promoting MSB events, running the MSB YouTube channel, maintaining the website's content, and writing quarterly newsletters. The two officers are expected to work closely together, splitting responsibilities to optimize effectiveness. Applications should indicate whether the candidate prefers focusing on social media management or the general online presence of MSB.

Social Events Officers (Two)

Responsible for planning and executing social events and cultural activities during the annual MICCAI conference. This includes organizing the evening and full-day tours and ensuring their smooth operation. Ideal candidates will be available to attend MICCAI 2026 in Abu Dhabi, UAE.

Executive and Financial Officer (One)

Responsible for managing the organization's finances, budgeting, and administrative tasks to ensure efficient operations and supporting the other MSB sub-groups. Early PhD students or Master's students are preferred candidates as this is an ideal entry position.

Local Student Liaison Officer (One)

Responsible for supporting the organization of local events during MICCAI 2026. Preference will be given to candidates with knowledge of UAE, particularly Abu Dhabi, and proficiency in Arabic.

Note: The other positions that are not vacant this year are: Professional Events Officer, Educational Initiatives Officer, Web Conference Officer and Sports Events Officer.
